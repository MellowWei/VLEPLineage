Eastern Lineage · 01
老子 · 道德经Laozi · Daodejing · circa 6th century BCE
Key Move · 关键动作

道可道,非常道;名可名,非常名。— 道德经 · 第一章 · Daodejing, Chapter 1 老子的核心动作:命名(可道)和真实存在(常道)分开。语言一旦说出 X,X 已经不是真正的 X。语言被诊断为必然失真的中介。

解决方案:不说(无名)。"无名,天地之始;有名,万物之母。"通过拒绝语言操作来保留存在的本真。 Laozi's core move: naming (the speakable) and true existence (the eternal) are split. The moment language utters X, X is no longer truly X. Language is diagnosed as a necessarily distorting medium.

Solution: do not speak (the nameless). Through refusing linguistic operation, the authenticity of existence is preserved.

关键差别:老子的"道"不可说,本学派的"本体"可以本体地说出来 —— "这就是"。Opposite direction, similar structure.

Key difference: Laozi's Dao cannot be spoken; this school's ontology can be ontologically spoken — "this is."

Eastern Lineage · 02
龙树 · 中观八不Nāgārjuna · Mūlamadhyamakakārikā · circa 2nd century CE
Key Move · 关键动作

不生亦不灭,不常亦不断,不一亦不异,不来亦不出。— 中论 · 八不偈 · Opening Verse 龙树《中论》的核心动作:通过否定所有二元对立来显形"空"。八不 = 不生/不灭 + 不常/不断 + 不一/不异 + 不来/不出。每一对都既不肯定也不否定,而是同时取消两极。

方法叫"破"(prasaṅga):不立任何命题,只破他人的命题。最终目的是让所有概念性 grasping(执)崩塌,显形空性(śūnyatā)。 Nāgārjuna's core move: manifest "emptiness" by negating all dualistic oppositions. Each pair is simultaneously canceled.

The method is called prasaṅga: establish no proposition; only refute others'. The final aim is to collapse all conceptual grasping, manifesting emptiness (śūnyatā).

同样取消二分,但取消之后的结果不同。

龙树:取消所有二分 → 留下空(无可说)

这是两个学派最深的不同 —— 空 vs 本体。

家族度评估:龙树是本学派最近的东方亲属(共享 14 二分柱的取消方法学),但终点完全不同。如果说本学派和老子是"反向同构",那么本学派和龙树是"同向异果"。Same dualism-dissolution, different post-dissolution result.

Nāgārjuna: dissolve all dualisms → leave emptiness (unspeakable)
This school: dissolve all dualisms → leave ontology (speakable, but only ontologically)

Family-resemblance assessment: Nāgārjuna is this school's closest Eastern kin in methodology, but end-points fully differ. Relation: "same-direction-different-result."

Eastern Lineage · 03
禅宗 · 截断众流Chan / Zen Buddhism · 6th–10th century CE
Key Move · 关键动作

不立文字,教外别传,直指人心,见性成佛。— 禅宗四句教 禅宗的核心动作:截断众流 —— 在概念思维流的中间直接打断,通过非语言动作(棒喝、公案、机锋)让主体当下脱出言语缠绕。

典型例子:临济义玄"无位真人是个什么干屎橛";赵州狗子"无";百丈野鸭子。每一个动作都是对语言操作的语言外打断。

禅宗对语言的态度比老子和龙树都激进:不只是"不说"或"破说",而是在说的过程中直接截断说本身。 Chan's core move: cut the stream — interrupt conceptual thinking mid-stream through non-linguistic acts (staff blow, koan, turn-word).

Examples: Linji's "true person of no rank — what dried shit-stick?"; Zhaozhou's "Wu"; Baizhang's wild duck. Each act is non-linguistic interruption of linguistic operation.

Chan's stance on language is more radical than Laozi's or Nāgārjuna's: not "do not speak" or "refute speech," but cut speaking itself in the middle of speaking.

禅宗的方法是外科手术(从外部打断);本学派的方法是语言内化疗(从内部重组)。

关键差别:禅宗最终走向语言之外(觉),本学派最终走向语言之精确(本体)。如果禅宗是"出言语"的极致,本学派是"入言语"的极致。Same recognition that "language has physical force," completely different response strategies.

Chan: language has force → use stronger non-linguistic force to cut it
This school: language has force → use precise linguistic-internal translation to reset it

Chan's method is surgical; this school's method is linguistic-internal chemotherapy.

Key difference: Chan moves outside language; this school moves into language's precision. If Chan is the apex of "exiting speech," this school is the apex of "entering speech."

Eastern Lineage · 04
维摩诘 · 不二法门Vimalakīrti · Dharma-Gate of Non-Duality · 1st–2nd century CE
Key Move · 关键动作

文殊师利问维摩诘:"何等是菩萨入不二法门?"时维摩诘默然无言。文殊师利叹曰:"善哉!乃至无有文字语言,是真入不二法门。"— 维摩诘所说经 · 入不二法门品 维摩诘的核心动作:默然 —— 文殊师利问"什么是不二法门",维摩诘不回答。这一沉默被文殊师利赞为"真入不二"。

这是东方传统里对"语言取消二分"的最极致演示:不只是说出取消(老子)、不只是辩证地破二分(龙树)、不只是非语言截断(禅),而是在被要求说出二分取消的瞬间用沉默本身完成取消。

沉默不是无语,是最高级的语言操作。 Vimalakīrti's core move: silent thunder — when asked "What is non-duality?", Vimalakīrti does not answer. This silence is praised as "truly entering non-duality."

This is the most extreme demonstration of "language dissolving dualism" in the Eastern tradition: using silence itself to complete the dissolution at the moment one is asked to speak the dissolution.

Silence is not absence of language — it is the highest linguistic operation.

家族度评估:维摩诘和本学派在自指方法学上最近 —— 比老子、龙树、禅都近,因为唯有维摩诘理解"学派必须以自己的方法立自己"。这是公理 7 在 2 世纪东方传统中的早期显形(尽管维摩诘没把它显形为公理,而是显形为一个故事)。Same requirement of "school self-demonstration," but inverse demonstration form.

Family-resemblance assessment: Vimalakīrti is the closest to this school in self-referential methodology — closer than Laozi, Nāgārjuna, or Chan — because only Vimalakīrti understood that "the school must establish itself by its own method." Early Eastern manifestation of Axiom 7 in the 2nd century.
